# Example Insights

- Premium beauty and gifting brands generated the strongest revenue contribution during peak campaign periods.
- Email and loyalty-driven channels showed higher conversion efficiency than broad awareness channels.
- International leisure travelers showed a higher average basket value than commuter segments.
- Some campaigns generated strong reach but weak conversions, suggesting creative or offer optimization opportunities.

## Business Recommendation
Focus future brand activations on higher-converting channels, strengthen campaigns for high-value traveler segments, and review underperforming campaigns with strong reach but low transaction yield.
